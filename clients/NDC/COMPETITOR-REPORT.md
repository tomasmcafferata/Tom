# Competitive Landscape Report: NDC (enedece.com.ar)
**Research date:** March 2026
**Market:** Argentine integrated visual communications — trade show stands, fleet wrapping, signage, event production

---

## 1. NDC — Subject Company Profile

**URL:** https://enedece.com.ar
**LinkedIn:** https://ar.linkedin.com/company/ndc-communication-group
**Founded:** 2009 (per LinkedIn)
**Size:** 2–10 employees (LinkedIn)
**HQ:** Valentín Vergara 3727, Buenos Aires (GBA north zone)

### Services (confirmed from search indexing of site pages)
- Arquitectura publicitaria / stands for ferias, exposiciones, congresos
- Ploteo vehicular — flotas, autos, camionetas, camiones, helicópteros, ómnibus
- Cartelería comercial — corpóreos, gigantografías, backlights, displays, microperforados
- Restyling de locales comerciales, oficinas y marquesinas (single locations and chains)
- Producción de eventos corporativos — full logistics: gráfica, transporte, alojamiento, merchandising, arquitectura publicitaria, audiovisual, promotoras, seguridad, iluminación
- Publicidad digital / social media

### Positioning Language (extracted from indexed content)
- "Diseñan, crean y ejecutan... produciendo todo de forma integrada dentro de NDC"
- "Con renders, diseños, taller y equipos de impresión propios"
- "Más de 21 años de experiencia"
- "Planta propia de impresión digital"
- "Trabajar junto a sus clientes para hacer crecer su negocio con estrategias a medida"
- "Ideas frescas y un servicio de producción, logística e instalación de calidad"
- "Profesionales apasionados por la publicidad gráfica"

### Key Differentiator (NDC's actual moat)
NDC combines in a single SME: (1) own print plant, (2) stand design + build, (3) fleet wrapping, (4) full event production, (5) office/retail restyling. This eliminates the need for a client to coordinate 3–5 separate vendors. No other Argentine competitor identified in this research covers all five verticals simultaneously at SME scale with in-house production.

**Gaps NDC uniquely fills (no other single company covers all of these):**

2. Fleet wrapping + stand building in one company — clients with both needs must use two vendors with everyone except NDC (and Fe Publicidad, partially).
3. Event production + in-house print + stand build — NDC handles the entire event ecosystem including logistics, AV, staffing, and gráfica without outsourcing.
4. Digital/social media + physical production — no stand competitor offers digital marketing alongside physical comms.

## 8. Competitive Positioning Recommendations for NDC

Based on this landscape, the sharpest positioning available to NDC:

**The gap:** Every competitor is a specialist. NDC is the only SME-scale integrated visual communications company that brings design, printing, stand construction, fleet wrapping, office restyling, and event production under one roof with a planta propia.

**Recommended positioning angle:** "Un solo interlocutor para toda tu comunicación visual" — one contact, one brief, one invoice, from the feria stand to the fleet to the store front. The client brief and brand guidelines go in once; everything comes out consistent.

**Proof points to foreground:**
- 21 years in market
- Planta propia de impresión digital (not outsourced)
- Explicit service list showing the 5 verticals
- Before/after restyling cases (unique differentiator)
- Fleet projects with brand consistency examples

**Language to avoid:** "Llave en mano" — completely commoditized. Every competitor uses it.

**Language to test:** "Comunicación visual integrada," "todo en casa," "una marca, una voz, un equipo," "del stand a la flota."
