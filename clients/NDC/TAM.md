## Executive Summary

NDC's total addressable market in Argentina spans three overlapping segments: trade show stand production, vehicle fleet wrapping, and retail/office visual communications (signage, restyling, cartelería). The combined TAM across these three categories for B2B companies in Argentina is estimated at **$3.5B–$7B ARS per year** (~$3.5M–$7M USD at current rates). NDC's realistic Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) in year 1 of active outbound — targeting Tier 1 and Tier 2 accounts in GBA — is **$120M–$400M ARS** ($120K–$400K USD), growing to **$500M–$1B+ ARS** by year 3 with a full outbound system and retainer model in place.

### Segment 2: Vehicle Fleet Wrapping

```
FLEET WRAPPING MARKET — ARGENTINA
===================================

Fleet wrapping universe (verified data):
  Total circulating vehicle fleet in Argentina (end 2024): 15,552,418 vehicles [AFAC]
    - Cars (82%):                   ~12.75M
    - Light commercial (15%):       ~2.33M  ← primary fleet wrapping universe
    - Heavy commercial (3%):        ~467K   ← trucks/buses
  Average fleet age: 14.3 years; fleet grew 1% in 2024

  ICP-fit fleets (companies with 5–100 branded vehicles, 20–300 employees):
    Transport/logistics SMEs (20–300 employees): ~4,700–5,000 companies [proxy: INDEC SIPA]
    Food & beverage/CPG with distribution fleets: ~2,000–3,000 companies
    Healthcare/pharma (rep/delivery vehicles): ~500–1,000 companies
    Retail chains (service/delivery fleets): ~1,000–2,000 companies
    TOTAL ICP-fit fleet companies: ~8,200–11,000 companies
    Estimated branded vehicles in ICP universe: ~100,000–250,000 vehicles

  Wrapping cycle: Vinyl fleet wraps last 3–5 years before replacement
  → Annual replacement market: ~20,000–83,000 vehicles/year within ICP
  → Conservative addressable (GBA-focused, SME): ~5,000–15,000 vehicles/year

  Note: 82% of logistics companies planned investments in 2025; 61% planned to hire
  [iProfesional] — sector is in active growth phase, a strong signal for new fleet branding.

Average project value:
  Single vehicle wrap (car/van):       $80,000–$150,000 ARS
  Truck/large vehicle wrap:            $150,000–$350,000 ARS
  Fleet contract (10 vehicles):        $800,000–$2,000,000 ARS
  Full fleet contract (50 vehicles):   $4,000,000–$8,000,000 ARS

  Weighted average per vehicle (installed): ~$120,000 ARS

TAM calculation (fleet segment):
  ICP-fit vehicles in annual replacement cycle: ~5,000–10,000
  × Weighted avg. per vehicle: ~$120,000 ARS
  = Fleet TAM (NDC-addressable): $600M–$1.2B ARS/year
                                  (~$600K–$1.2M USD)

  Note: Base3 (the dominant fleet specialist) operates at the large-enterprise end
  (Quilmes, Andreani, Red Bull). NDC's opportunity is the underserved SME/mid-market
  fleet segment (5–50 vehicles) where no integrated provider dominates.
```
